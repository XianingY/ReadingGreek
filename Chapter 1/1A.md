The ship is in Byzantium.
(And) in the Byzantium, Hegestratos goes onto the ship, then Sdenothemis goes onto the ship, finally the captain and the sailors embark onto the ship.
(And) the ship sails to Chios.
(And) in Chios, the rhapsode embarks (onto the ship).
And then the ship sails to Euboia.
(And) in Euboia, Dikaiopolis embarks (onto the ship).
And finally the ship sails towards Athens and towards the Peiraieus.

(Therefore, on the one hand) the ship sails, (on the other hand) Sdnothemis looks towards the land.
What does Sdnothemis see?
Sdnothemis sees (both) the Acropolis and the Parthenon.
And then (both) Dikaiopolis and the captain look towards the land.
What do Dikaiopolis and the captain see?
Both Dikaiopolis and the captain see (both) the Acropolis and the Parthenon.
Suddenly (both) Dikaiopolis and the captain hear a noise.
